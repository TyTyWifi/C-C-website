ABOUT US

Service with a Heart

At Cactus & Coach, we believe luxury isn't just about the vehicle — it's about the experience.

Founded by Thomas Gateley, a seasoned professional with 10+ years of rideshare excellence and over 10,000 five-star trips, Cactus & Coach was created from a commitment to restore the extraordinary customer service that's been lost in today's transportation industry. Born in Scottsdale with 34 years in the Valley, Thomas combines deep local knowledge with world-class professionalism to deliver a truly elevated private SUV service.

But this isn't just a business — it's a passion with a purpose. By choosing Cactus & Coach, you're not only experiencing the best driver, in the best vehicle, at the best rates — you're also supporting his 2-year-old son, Ryan, who is courageously living with a disability that prevents him from walking. Every ride helps provide for Ryan's care and future.

When you ride with us, you're part of something bigger: exceptional service with a heart.
